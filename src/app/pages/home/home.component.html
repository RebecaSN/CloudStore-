Welcome to the Cloudonix Product Manager: Cloud-Store Manager

Cloud-Store Manager is dedicated to transforming the way businesses manage their products online. This product manager is designed to meet the need for fast, secure, and easy-to-use solutions, and is fully integrated with the Cloudonix platform.
With Cloud-Store Manager, your online store benefits from a system that automates and simplifies inventory management, allowing you to focus on what really matters: growing your business. The application you’re about to use allows you to manage your products intuitively and efficiently, thanks to the robust infrastructure of Cloudonix.
Instructions for Use:
1. Log in using your Cloudonix authorization key.
2. View, edit, or create new products with total flexibility.
3. Delete products securely, with confirmation for each deletion action.
